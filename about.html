It's fair to call Tunde Ednut jack of all trades. He had tried his hands on a couple of things. Tunde Ednut made an entry into the music industry after his university education abroad. Tunde Ednut started out as a comedian. In fact, he was asked to perform at the 2009 Dynamiks Award in Nigeria. His performance at the show was a huge success. Expectedly, his amazing performance at the award night didn't go unnoticed. He got invitations from the UK-based Black Nights Entertainment and Femi Amusan. Tunde Ednut's comedy career was looking very promising as he started performing alongside big names in the industry. He was known for his stage management and ability to make audiences crack their ribs. He was invited to the United Kingdom for shows, which began his journey into true stardom. At one point, Tunde Ednut was regarded as one of the best Nigerian comedians residing in the UK.

Tunde Olaoluwa Adekunle popularly known as Tunde Ednut was born on the 20th of January in Katsina State, Nigeria. His parents are said to be lecturers. He is the first son and second child of a family of five. Apparently, only little is known about Tunde Ednut family and his age. Tunde Ednut had his primary education at Cambridge International School and attended Federal Government College, Ugolawo in Kogi State for his secondary education. He studied Graphic Arts at the University of Lagos. Ednut also went to Kingsland University in London, the United Kingdom where he obtained a degree in Graphic Designs. He is famous for posting entertaining content on his Instagram account, ranging from comedy, movies to music videos. He had almost 3 million followers on his main account before it was blocked by Instagram.
